about

She's "Mom" to Lisa, Daniel, Marian and Samantha (and Jeff, Amy, and Rich too!), and "Grandma" to Trent, Siena, Celia, Harrison and Owen. Through the years, she's given them all so much love, laughter and support. Her determination (to get upgrades and access to airline lounges) has inspired them to never give up. She's fixed clothing and suitcases that seemed beyond salvation. She's “doctored up” egg salad fit for a queen. Her pavlova can’t be beat (although of course it must be beaten properly to form the meringue) and her salad dressing has inspired generations of Raymans in the 70’s and 80’s to declare artichokes as their favorite food (sending shockwaves through the school). Seeing her children and grandchildren happy is what makes her the happiest, and that is just one of the many things that make her so special. We love her so much, she's the best Mom and Grandma anyone could ask for.
